# InferLane Refund Policy

This policy is separate from the Dispute Resolution Policy. A
dispute is a contested workload; a refund is a direct request to
return prepaid funds.

## 1. Prepaid wallet balance refunds

Unused prepaid wallet balance is refundable on request, subject to:

- A $5 minimum refund amount (processing overhead).
- Refund via the original payment method within 10 business days.
- We deduct Stripe's payment processing fee from the refund amount
  (typically 2.9% + 30¢) since that fee was incurred on the
  original deposit.
- Funds held in escrow for in-flight workloads are not refundable
  until the workload completes or its dispute window expires.

To request a refund:

- **Dashboard**: Settings → Billing → Request Refund
- **Email**: billing@inferlane.dev with your user id and requested amount

## 2. Subscription refunds

### Pro and Supplier tiers

Monthly subscriptions are refundable on a pro-rated basis for the
unused portion of the current billing period. Annual subscriptions
are refundable within 30 days of the start of the annual term for
a full refund, and pro-rated thereafter for the unused months.

### Enterprise tier

Enterprise contracts govern refunds per their negotiated terms.
Standard terms permit termination for cause with a pro-rated
refund of any pre-paid but unused period. Termination for
convenience is not default — contact your account manager.

## 3. EU / UK consumer withdrawal right

Consumers in the European Economic Area and United Kingdom have a
14-day right of withdrawal under the Consumer Rights Directive /
Consumer Contracts Regulations. This right applies to:

- Subscription purchases
- Prepaid wallet deposits

The right does NOT apply to digital services you have already
consumed (executed workloads). If you withdraw within the 14-day
window you will be refunded for unused balance.

## 4. Australian Consumer Law

Consumers in Australia have statutory guarantees under the
Australian Consumer Law that cannot be excluded, including the
right to a refund for a major failure of the service. Nothing in
this policy limits those rights.

## 5. Non-refundable items

- Platform fees already collected on completed workloads
- Reserve fund contributions
- Fees already paid to upstream providers (those refund only at
  their discretion)
- Any amount we have already disbursed to an operator and cannot
  recover

## 6. Disputes vs refunds

If you believe a specific workload was defective or the operator
misrepresented their capabilities, **open a dispute** rather than
a refund request. Dispute resolution draws from the reserve fund
and does not require the operator to cooperate. See the Dispute
Resolution Policy for details.

## 7. Refund processing time

| Payment method | Typical time |
|---|---|
| Credit / debit card | 5–10 business days |
| ACH / bank transfer | 7–14 business days |
| USDT on Plasma | < 1 hour |
| USDT on Ethereum | 15–60 minutes plus on-chain fees |

## 8. Disputes about refunds

If we decline a refund request, you may:

- Escalate to the dispute committee at disputes@inferlane.dev
- For consumers in the EU/UK, lodge a complaint with your local
  consumer protection authority or Online Dispute Resolution
  platform: https://ec.europa.eu/consumers/odr
- For consumers in Australia, contact the ACCC or your state's
  consumer affairs office

## 9. Changes

Material changes announced at least 30 days in advance via
CHANGELOG and customer email.
